title=How to add, edit and remove Components
mode=3
text=Components let you use some functionality without scripting. They are standard MonoBehaviour scripts however they have custom tree view in Inspector (you can turn it off, if you want). Check Components gallery for available components. <br><br> Chose 'Components' tab and hit plus icon to add a component. Some components depend on others and will be added automatically. Change settings in the Inspector. Click on Delete icon to remove.
